Tokyo to Kawaguchiko Express

The essential first-timer's Fuji day trip. Hit the best viewpoints, enjoy local cuisine, and be back in Tokyo for dinner.

7:00 AM

Depart Shinjuku Station

Take the direct highway bus to Kawaguchiko (2 hours)

9:00 AM

Arrive Kawaguchiko

Pick up the retro bus day pass at the station

9:30 AM

Chureito Pagoda

Climb 400 steps for the iconic pagoda view (1.5 hours)

12:00 PM

Lunch in Fujiyoshida

Try local Yoshida udon noodles

1:30 PM

Lake Kawaguchiko North Shore

Walk or rent bikes along the lakeside path

4:00 PM

Panoramic views from Mt Tenjo observation deck

5:30 PM

Return to Tokyo

Evening bus back to Shinjuku (2 hours)

Planning Tips

Make the most of your Fuji trip

Book Early

Ryokan and popular restaurants fill up quickly, especially during cherry blossom and autumn foliage seasons.

Check Weather

Fuji visibility can be unpredictable. Have backup plans and be flexible with your schedule.

Get Passes

The Fuji Hakone Pass or retro bus day pass can save money and simplify transportation.
